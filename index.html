Introduction

Hello! My name is Hanbo. I am a student pursuing a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Michigan. I have internship experience as a design verification engineer and I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from the Penn State University in May 2020. I am currently seeking a Design Verification Engineer position to begin my career in a high-level professional environment. If you take the time to scroll through my pages, you will see examples of my work, images of my projects, and how I am involved in school.

I am also an amateur photographer. I specialize in landscape and portrait photography, and I particularly enjoy shooting in dark environments and creating images with a "fairy tale" feel. My main equipment is a Canon 5D Mark III. I sometimes shoot with film and develop film myself. The film images are not as perfect as digital images, but the tone, softness and grain of my film work is something that I enjoy very much.
